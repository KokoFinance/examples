# Sample MCP Prompts for Koko Finance

Once you've connected the Koko Finance MCP server to Claude Desktop (see [setup guide](claude-desktop-setup.md)), try these prompts:

## Card Comparison

> "Compare the Chase Sapphire Preferred and the Capital One Venture X. Which is better for someone who spends $500/month on dining and $300/month on travel?"

> "How does the Amex Gold Card stack up against the Chase Sapphire Preferred for groceries?"

## Recommendations

> "I spend a lot on groceries and dining. What's the best credit card for me?"

> "Recommend a no-annual-fee travel card with good rewards."

> "What's the best cashback card right now?"

## Portfolio Analysis

> "I have these cards: Chase Sapphire Reserve, Amex Gold, and Citi Double Cash. Am I getting good value? Are there any gaps in my portfolio?"

> "Analyze my wallet: Chase Freedom Unlimited and Discover it. I spend about $400 on groceries, $300 on dining, and $200 on gas per month."

## Renewal Decisions

> "My Chase Sapphire Reserve annual fee is coming up. Is it worth keeping?"

> "Should I renew my Amex Platinum or downgrade to the Amex Gold?"

## Guided Workflows (Using Built-in Prompts)

Ask Claude to use the built-in prompts for a structured experience:

> "Use the portfolio-review prompt to help me analyze my cards"

> "Use the which-card prompt — I need to buy a laptop"

> "Use the new-card-finder prompt — I'm looking for a new travel card"
